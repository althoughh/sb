01 Challenge

Scaling without losing control of costs

Traveltek is a mature cloud customer, using a mixture of AWS services and technology to deliver their travel technology solutions. Their AWS usage is continually evolving to ensure it is performing optimally and adapting to meet customer needs, which meant they required an agile cloud reseller who could support their continually evolving requirements.

Traveltek was looking for a cloud FinOps partner who could optimize cloud costs in a non-restrictive and flexible way. This would enable the Traveltek team to focus on delivering the best possible solutions to their end-customers, safe in the knowledge that Strategic Blue are experts in cloud cost reduction and proactively identifying new savings opportunities.

Previous attempts at commitment optimization had resulted in either over-provisioning (wasting money) or under-provisioning (missing savings opportunities). They needed a partner who could manage this complexity proactively without requiring technical changes.

02 Solution

A careful blend of RIs and Savings Plans

The solution was clear. Traveltek needed an AWS reseller that understands how to optimize AWS costs using a careful blend of Reserved Instances and Savings Plans. Working closely together, Strategic Blue ensured that Traveltek's technical changes were fully compatible with existing commitments to maintain business agility whilst preventing wastage and overspend.

Strategic Blue also tailored Traveltek's dashboard to their exact reporting requirements, helping to identify key cloud consumption trends and accurately forecast AWS costs. Having access to this data has empowered Traveltek to promote a cost-focused mentality within the organisation.

Regular monthly meetings are in place to make sure both teams are on top of everything cloud cost related, whether that's exploring additional savings, cost reporting requirements or other cost-focused initiatives.

03 Results

Consistent savings, even as usage grew

Thanks to Strategic Blue's cloud FinOps expertise, Traveltek currently saves 12% on their monthly AWS bill. These cost savings have remained consistent despite changes to Traveltek's AWS infrastructure and an 8% increase in usage over the past year.

The Traveltek team now has additional time and budget which they use to focus on more important tasks, such as product development and delivering the best customer experience possible to their end-customers.

Traveltek enjoy having a trusted cloud FinOps partner who is proactively identifying savings opportunities so they don't have to spend time worrying about their bills. As Traveltek continues to grow, Strategic Blue's knowledge of the discount mechanisms in AWS and their ability to leverage these optimally will play an important role in Traveltek's success.
